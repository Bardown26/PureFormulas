About EquiLife

EquiLife is a wellness brand dedicated to providing premium, science-driven supplements that support overall health and vitality. With a focus on clean, high-quality ingredients, EquiLife offers a wide range of products designed to optimize gut health, immunity, energy, detoxification, and more. The brand prioritizes transparency and rigorous testing, ensuring every product meets the highest standards of purity and effectiveness. EquiLife’s mission is to empower individuals to take control of their health with trusted, evidence-based solutions that promote long-term wellness and a balanced lifestyle.

Frequently Asked Questions

EquiLife is based in Walpole, Massachusetts. While specific manufacturing sites are not publicly listed, EquiLife partners with reputable facilities that adhere to strict quality protocols to ensure product consistency and safety.

EquiLife focuses on comprehensive wellness support, offering an extensive selection of supplements, functional lab tests, and personalized health programs. Their offerings are designed to help maintain balance in everyday health routines, with options that suit a variety of wellness needs.

EquiLife stands out for integrating time-tested wellness traditions with modern science. Their formulas reflect a holistic view of health, and the brand’s commitment to clean, allergen-conscious ingredients—free from common irritants like dairy, gluten, and soy—makes them a strong option for those with sensitivities or seeking a cleaner supplement profile.

Quality is at the heart of EquiLife’s mission. Every product is developed using high-quality raw materials and undergoes rigorous testing for purity and potency. Their manufacturing partners follow stringent standards to ensure consistency and reliability in every formula.
