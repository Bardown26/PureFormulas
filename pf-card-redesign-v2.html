V2 — What's different from V1

This version strips the card down to its simplest effective form. No toggle, no mode switching, no shipping line.

  • Removed: Autoship / One-time toggle — the card shows one price (the standard price) with the autoship offer below it
  • Removed: "Fast Free Shipping" — redundant when every card shows it; adds noise without information
  • Added: Green autoship callout bar with clock icon — shows the autoship price and dollar savings in one line
  • Kept: Trust line ("Skip, change, or cancel anytime") — still the #1 lever for subscription hesitancy
  • Kept: Standard "Add to Cart" CTA — autoship enrollment happens at checkout, but now the user enters with full awareness of the offer

Trade-off vs V1:

V1 with the toggle lets users commit to autoship directly from the card (higher intent signal, potentially higher autoship conversion). V2 is simpler — it surfaces the autoship value prop clearly but defers the subscribe/one-time decision to checkout. The right choice depends on whether PureFormulas wants the card to sell autoship or inform about autoship.
